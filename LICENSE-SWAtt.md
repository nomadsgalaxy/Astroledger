# Open Community License - Software Attribution (SWAtt v1)

SWAtt serves solely as an add-on condition for OCL distribution and is effective only if specifically referenced in said OCL distribution (`OCL v* + SWAtt v1` or `Open Community License v* + Software Attribution v1`). Such add-on only adds further binding license condition without any change or invalidation of any part of OCL.

## As a non-commercial END USER

If you distribute your creations, modifications, or any such derivatives YOU MUST reasonably and in a common manner retain and represent in your distribution the identification of the creator/s of the product and/or its components and its derivatives on which your derivative is based.

YOU MUST represent said identification of the creator/s visibly for the user in the user interface of your derivative and in the source code of your derivative. User interface representation may be omitted ONLY if your derivative lacks user interface, and therefore does not provide software means for data input and/or output readout by a user. Representation SHOULD include "built on [identification of creator/s]".

In respect to practical representation limitations YOU MUST retain and represent AT LEAST the identification of the creator/s of the product and/or its components, and if applicable the identification of the creator/s of the last derivative, on which your derivative is based.
